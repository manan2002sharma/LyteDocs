Claiming Rewards

When your total pending rewards (cashback + referral earnings) reach the minimum claim threshold, you can withdraw them to your wallet.

How to Claim​

  1. Open the main menu
  2. Tap Rewards (or send /referral)
  3. If your balance meets the minimum, tap Claim Rewards
  4. Confirm the claim

Your rewards are sent directly to your Safe wallet in USDC.

Minimum Claim Amount​

There is a minimum claimable amount to prevent dust transactions. The threshold is shown on the rewards dashboard.

If your balance is below the minimum, the button shows Claim (Min $X) and is locked until you reach the threshold.

Claim Processing​

Claims are processed as gasless transactions via Polymarket's relay infrastructure. No Polygon gas fees are charged to you.

Processing typically takes 10–30 seconds.

Reward Ledger​

The dashboard tracks:

FieldDescription
GeneratedTotal rewards earned (lifetime)
WithdrawnTotal rewards claimed (lifetime)
PendingGenerated minus Withdrawn = available now

Pending rewards = what you can claim today.

Notes​

  • Cashback and referral rewards are pooled together for claiming — you claim both in one transaction.
  • Rewards are denominated in USD value. The equivalent USDC is sent to your Safe.
  • After claiming, your withdrawn amounts update immediately in the dashboard.
  • Rewards are funded by the platform from trading fees — no external token or staking required.

Refreshing the Dashboard​

Tap Refresh on the rewards screen to see the latest real-time balance. Rewards update after every trade you or your referrals make.
