# Cashback & Referral Rewards

Lyte rewards you for trading and for inviting others. Every fee paid on the platform generates rewards — for you and for whoever referred you.

---

## How Rewards Work

### Cashback (Every Trade)

Every time you trade, you earn **10% of the platform fee** back as cashback.

| You trade | Platform fee (0.5%) | Your cashback (10% of fee) |
|-----------|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| $100 | $0.50 | **$0.05** |
| $1,000 | $5.00 | **$0.50** |
| $10,000 | $50.00 | **$5.00** |

Cashback accumulates in your rewards balance and can be claimed at any time.

---

### Referral Rewards (L1 — Direct Referrals)

When you refer someone and they trade, you earn **60% of every fee they pay**.

| Your referral trades | Fee generated | You earn (60%) |
|---------------------|---------------|----------------|
| $100 | $0.50 | **$0.30** |
| $1,000 | $5.00 | **$3.00** |
| $10,000 | $50.00 | **$30.00** |

---

### Second-Degree Referrals (L2)

When your referrals refer others, you earn **10% of every fee** from those second-degree users.

Example chain: **You → Alice → Bob**
- Bob makes a trade generating $1.00 fee
- Alice earns $0.60 (L1, direct referral)
- **You earn $0.10** (L2, second degree)
- Bob earns $0.10 (10% cashback)

---

## Fee Distribution Summary

| Scenario | Cashback | L1 Referral | L2 Referral | Platform |
|----------|----------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| No referrer | 10% | — | — | **90%** |
| Has L1 referrer | 10% | 60% | — | **30%** |
| Has L1 + L2 chain | 10% | 60% | 10% | **20%** |

The platform always keeps a minimum of **20%** of every fee.

## Viewing Your Rewards Dashboard

1. Open the main menu
2. Tap **Rewards** (or send `/referral`)

The dashboard shows:
- **Cashback generated** — total cashback earned from your own trades
- **Cashback withdrawn** — amount already claimed
- **Cashback pending** — available to claim now
- **Referral earnings** — total earned from L1 + L2 referrals
- **Referred users** — how many people you've directly referred
- **Total claimable** — cashback + referral earnings ready to claim
- **Your referral link** — share this to earn referral commissions

---

## Your Referral Link

Your unique referral link looks like:

```
https://t.me/YourBotUsername?start=r_ABC12345
```

Share it anywhere — Telegram, Twitter, Discord. Anyone who clicks it and completes setup is automatically linked to you.

---

## Referral Attribution Rules

- The referral link must be used during the `/start` setup flow to be attributed.
- If someone was already a user before clicking your link, no attribution is applied.
- Self-referral is not prevented but earns nothing meaningful (you'd just be paying yourself).
