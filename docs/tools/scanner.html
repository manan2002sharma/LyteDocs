Trader Scanner

The Trader Scanner lets you look up any Polymarket wallet address and see its full trading history and performance stats.

Using the Scanner​

  1. Open the main menu
  2. Tap Scan (or send /scan)
  3. Enter a wallet address (e.g. 0x1234...abcd)

The bot fetches and displays:

  • Total volume traded
  • Profit / Loss (USD)
  • Win rate
  • Number of markets traded
  • Recent trade history

What It's Useful For​

  • Due diligence — before copying a wallet, research its full history
  • Research specific traders — look up addresses you've seen trading large positions
  • Verify performance — confirm the stats match what you saw on the leaderboard
  • Monitor yourself — look up your own Polymarket wallet to see your stats

Difference from Leaderboard​

FeatureLeaderboardScanner
Who it showsTop-ranked traders onlyAny wallet you enter
Use caseDiscover top tradersResearch a specific wallet
Input requiredNoneWallet address

Taking Action on Scanned Wallets​

After scanning a wallet, you can:

  • Track Wallet — add to your Wallet Tracker for real-time notifications
  • Copy Trade — start mirroring their trades automatically

Notes​

  • The scanner queries Polymarket's public Data API. Any wallet that has traded on Polymarket can be looked up.
  • Stats are based on historical on-chain data and may have a short delay.
