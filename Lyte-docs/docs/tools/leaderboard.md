# Leaderboard

The Leaderboard lets you discover the top-performing Polymarket traders, view their stats, and start copying or tracking them.

---

## Opening the Leaderboard

1. Open the main menu
2. Tap **Leaderboard** (or send `/leaderboard`)

---

## What You'll See

The leaderboard ranks traders by profit/performance. For each trader:

- Wallet address (truncated)
- Total volume traded
- Profit/Loss (USD)
- Win rate
- Number of trades

---

## Trader Profiles

Tap any trader to open their full profile:

- Complete trading stats
- Recent trades
- Markets they're active in
- P&L breakdown

From a trader's profile you can:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Copy Trade** | Start automatically mirroring their trades |
| **Track Wallet** | Get notified whenever they trade (via Wallet Tracker) |
| **Share Profile** | Generate a deep link to share their profile |

---

## Deep Links

Leaderboard profiles are shareable via Telegram deep links. When someone taps a shared profile link, it opens directly in the bot — no searching required.

---

## Leaderboard Period

The leaderboard reflects recent trading performance. Rankings update periodically based on Polymarket's data.

---

## Finding Copy Trade Targets

The leaderboard is the recommended way to find wallets worth copying. Look for traders with:
- High win rate (above 60%)
- Consistent volume (not just a few lucky trades)
- Diverse markets (not concentrated in one position)

Once you identify a target, tap **Copy Trade** on their profile to set up automatic copying. See: [Copy Trading](../automation/copy-trading.md)
